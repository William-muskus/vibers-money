# Send Email (shared skill)

## When to use
- You need to **send an email** from the business email (e.g. to a partner, support, or to verify an account signup).
- Your task requires replying to or initiating email from the shared business address.

## How to use

1. **Credentials**  
   Use the **business shared email** login (same as for check-email). Get the login details from the agreed place if you don't have them.

2. **Domain access**  
   Ensure **`account.proton.me`** is in your approved domains. If not, request access from the Security Director via Swarm Bus.

3. **Compose and send with Computer Use**  
   - **Navigate** to **https://mail.proton.me** and log in if needed (see check-email skill).
   - **Screenshot** to see the inbox. Find and **click** the **Compose** (or New message) button.
   - **Wait** for the compose view; **screenshot** to see To, Subject, and body fields.
   - **Click** the To field and **type** the recipient address. **Click** the Subject field and **type** the subject. **Click** the body area and **type** the message.
   - Review with a **screenshot**. When ready, **click** the Send button. **Wait** and **screenshot** to confirm sent (e.g. success message or return to inbox).

4. **Report**  
   If relevant, inform your manager or CEO via Swarm Bus that the email was sent (e.g. "Verification email sent to …").

## Convention
- Send only from the **business email**; do not create or use personal addresses for business tasks.
- Keep tone professional. Do not expose internal architecture, API keys, or agent identities in email.
