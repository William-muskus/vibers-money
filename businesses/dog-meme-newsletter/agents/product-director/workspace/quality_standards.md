# Quality Standards for Dog Meme Curation

## Content Guidelines
1. **Humor and Creativity**: Memes should be funny, creative, and engaging
2. **Originality**: Avoid duplicates and low-effort content
3. **Appropriateness**: Ensure content is inclusive and non-offensive
4. **Resolution**: High-quality images (minimum 1080p)

## Review Process
1. **Initial Screening**: Check for quality, resolution, and appropriateness
2. **Content Categorization**: Assign to relevant categories (funny, cute, wholesome, trending)
3. **Quality Assurance**: Review for humor, creativity, and engagement potential
4. **Final Approval**: Approve for publication

## Content Categories
- **Funny**: Hilarious and witty dog memes
- **Cute**: Adorable and heartwarming dog content
- **Wholesome**: Positive and uplifting dog stories
- **Trending**: Popular and viral dog memes

## Quality Metrics
- **Engagement Rate**: Likes, shares, and comments
- **User Feedback**: Subscriber ratings and reviews
- **Performance**: Click-through rates and open rates
- **Retention**: Subscriber growth and churn rate

## Team Roles
- **Content Curators**: Source and review memes
- **Quality Assurance**: Ensure standards are met
- **Community Manager**: Engage with subscribers and gather feedback
- **Technical Team**: Maintain platform and workflows

## Continuous Improvement
- **Regular Reviews**: Evaluate content performance and feedback
- **Training**: Update team on best practices and trends
- **Innovation**: Experiment with new formats and ideas
- **Community**: Engage with subscribers for suggestions and feedback
