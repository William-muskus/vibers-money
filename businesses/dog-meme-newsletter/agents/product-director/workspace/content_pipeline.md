# Content Pipeline and Workflows

## Content Sourcing
1. **Social Media Monitoring**: Track trending dog memes on platforms like Reddit, Twitter, Instagram, and TikTok
2. **User Submissions**: Allow community members to submit their own dog memes
3. **Partnerships**: Collaborate with dog influencers and meme creators
4. **Original Content**: Create in-house dog memes and content

## Content Review Process
1. **Initial Screening**: Check for quality, resolution, and appropriateness
2. **Content Categorization**: Assign to relevant categories (funny, cute, wholesome, trending)
3. **Quality Assurance**: Review for humor, creativity, and engagement potential
4. **Final Approval**: Approve for publication

## Publication Workflow
1. **Daily Curation**: Select top memes for the day's newsletter
2. **Newsletter Assembly**: Compile memes into a structured format
3. **Testing**: Ensure compatibility across devices and email clients
4. **Distribution**: Send to subscribers via email

## Tools and Platforms
- **Content Management**: Use a CMS to organize and track memes
- **Email Service**: Reliable ESP for newsletter delivery
- **Analytics**: Track engagement and performance metrics
- **Community Platform**: Engage with subscribers and gather feedback

## Quality Standards
- **Resolution**: High-quality images (minimum 1080p)
- **Content**: Appropriate, inclusive, and non-offensive
- **Originality**: Avoid duplicates and low-effort content
- **Engagement**: Memes should be humorous, creative, and shareable

## Team Roles
- **Content Curators**: Source and review memes
- **Quality Assurance**: Ensure standards are met
- **Community Manager**: Engage with subscribers and gather feedback
- **Technical Team**: Maintain platform and workflows
