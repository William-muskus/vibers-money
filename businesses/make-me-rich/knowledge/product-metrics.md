# Product Metrics and KPIs: make-me-rich

## Overview
This document defines the key performance indicators (KPIs) and metrics for measuring the success of the make-me-rich platform.

## Business Objectives
1. **User Acquisition**: Attract and retain users
2. **Platform Adoption**: Drive platform usage and engagement
3. **Business Growth**: Enable business creation and growth
4. **Revenue Generation**: Generate sustainable revenue
5. **Operational Excellence**: Ensure platform reliability and performance

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### 1. User Acquisition Metrics

#### User Growth
- **Metric**: Number of new users per month
- **Target**: 100 new users/month (initial target)
- **Measurement**: Monthly active users (MAU)
- **Data Source**: User database, authentication logs

#### User Retention
- **Metric**: User retention rate (1-month, 3-month, 6-month)
- **Target**: 70% retention at 1 month, 50% at 3 months
- **Measurement**: Percentage of users returning after initial signup
- **Data Source**: User activity logs, engagement metrics

#### Conversion Rate
- **Metric**: Conversion rate from trial to paid
- **Target**: 20% conversion rate
- **Measurement**: Percentage of trial users converting to paid plans
- **Data Source**: Payment processing logs, user accounts

### 2. Platform Adoption Metrics

#### Platform Engagement
- **Metric**: Daily Active Users (DAU) and Monthly Active Users (MAU)
- **Target**: DAU/MAU ratio of 0.3 (30% daily engagement)
- **Measurement**: Number of unique users active per day/month
- **Data Source**: User activity logs, session tracking

#### Feature Adoption
- **Metric**: Feature adoption rate (percentage of users using key features)
- **Target**: 80% adoption of core features within 3 months
- **Measurement**: Percentage of users utilizing specific features
- **Data Source**: Feature usage logs, analytics data

#### Session Duration
- **Metric**: Average session duration
- **Target**: 15 minutes per session
- **Measurement**: Average time spent per user session
- **Data Source**: Session tracking data, user activity logs

### 3. Business Growth Metrics

#### Business Creation
- **Metric**: Number of businesses launched per month
- **Target**: 50 businesses/month (initial target)
- **Measurement**: Count of new business entities created
- **Data Source**: Business database, platform logs

#### Business Success Rate
- **Metric**: Percentage of businesses achieving revenue
- **Target**: 60% of businesses generating revenue within 6 months
- **Measurement**: Percentage of businesses with positive revenue
- **Data Source**: Business analytics, revenue tracking

#### Business Growth
- **Metric**: Average revenue growth per business
- **Target**: 20% monthly revenue growth per business
- **Measurement**: Monthly revenue growth rate per business
- **Data Source**: Business financial data, revenue reports

### 4. Revenue Generation Metrics

#### Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- **Metric**: Monthly Recurring Revenue
- **Target**: $50,000 MRR within 12 months
- **Measurement**: Total recurring revenue from subscriptions
- **Data Source**: Payment processing system, billing logs

#### Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)
- **Metric**: Average Revenue Per User
- **Target**: $20 ARPU
- **Measurement**: Total revenue divided by number of users
- **Data Source**: Payment processing data, user accounts

#### Churn Rate
- **Metric**: Customer churn rate
- **Target**: Less than 5% monthly churn
- **Measurement**: Percentage of users canceling subscriptions
- **Data Source**: Billing logs, user account status

### 5. Operational Excellence Metrics

#### Platform Uptime
- **Metric**: Platform availability and uptime
- **Target**: 99.9% uptime
- **Measurement**: Percentage of time platform is operational
- **Data Source**: Monitoring systems, uptime logs

#### System Performance
- **Metric**: System response time and throughput
- **Target**: Response time < 500ms for 95% of requests
- **Measurement**: Average response time and request throughput
- **Data Source**: Performance monitoring, load testing

#### Error Rate
- **Metric**: System error rate
- **Target**: Less than 0.1% error rate
- **Measurement**: Percentage of requests resulting in errors
- **Data Source**: Error logging, monitoring systems

#### Agent Performance
- **Metric**: Agent task completion rate and success rate
- **Target**: 95% task completion rate, 90% success rate
- **Measurement**: Percentage of tasks completed successfully
- **Data Source**: Task tracking logs, agent performance data

## Success Criteria

### Short-term (0-6 months)
- Achieve 100 new users/month
- 70% user retention at 1 month
- Launch 50 businesses/month
- $10,000 MRR
- 99.9% platform uptime

### Medium-term (6-12 months)
- Achieve 200 new users/month
- 60% user retention at 3 months
- Launch 100 businesses/month
- $50,000 MRR
- 20% monthly revenue growth per business

### Long-term (12+ months)
- Achieve 500 new users/month
- 50% user retention at 6 months
- Launch 200 businesses/month
- $100,000 MRR
- 30% monthly revenue growth per business
