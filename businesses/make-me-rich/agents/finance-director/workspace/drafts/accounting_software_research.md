# Free Accounting Software Research

## Top Free Options

### 1. Wave Apps
- **Features**: Invoicing, accounting, receipt scanning
- **Limitations**: Limited payroll features
- **Best for**: Small businesses, freelancers
- **Website**: https://www.waveapps.com

### 2. Zoho Books
- **Features**: Invoicing, expense tracking, reporting
- **Limitations**: Free plan limited to 1,000 invoices/year
- **Best for**: Startups, small businesses
- **Website**: https://www.zoho.com/books

### 3. GnuCash
- **Features**: Double-entry accounting, reports
- **Limitations**: Desktop-only, learning curve
- **Best for**: Personal finance, small businesses
- **Website**: https://www.gnucash.org

### 4. Akaunting
- **Features**: Open-source, cloud/self-hosted
- **Limitations**: Requires setup for self-hosted
- **Best for**: Developers, tech-savvy users
- **Website**: https://akaunting.com

### 5. QuickBooks Online (Free Trial)
- **Features**: Full accounting suite
- **Limitations**: Not free long-term
- **Best for**: Testing before commitment
- **Website**: https://quickbooks.intuit.com

## Recommendation
**Wave Apps** - Best balance of features and ease of use for zero-budget operations. No credit card required for signup.
