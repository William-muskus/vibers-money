# Affiliate Program Research

## Top Financial Affiliate Programs

### 1. Stripe
- **Commission**: 20-30% recurring
- **Cookie**: 90 days
- **Payout**: Monthly
- **Best for**: Payment processing, SaaS businesses

### 2. PayPal
- **Commission**: 10-20% per referral
- **Cookie**: 30 days
- **Payout**: Net 30
- **Best for**: Freelancers, small businesses

### 3. Robinhood
- **Commission**: $5-$50 per sign-up
- **Cookie**: 45 days
- **Payout**: Bi-weekly
- **Best for**: Investing audience

### 4. SoFi
- **Commission**: $50-$200 per loan
- **Cookie**: 60 days
- **Payout**: Monthly
- **Best for**: Personal finance, loans

### 5. Chime
- **Commission**: $50-$100 per account
- **Cookie**: 45 days
- **Payout**: Monthly
- **Best for**: Banking services
