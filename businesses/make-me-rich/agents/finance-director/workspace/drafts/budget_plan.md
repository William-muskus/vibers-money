## Risk Management

### Financial Risks

1. **Revenue Risk**
   - Lower than expected customer acquisition
   - Higher than expected churn rate
   - Pricing pressure from competitors

2. **Cost Risk**
   - Higher than budgeted salaries
   - Unexpected infrastructure costs
   - Legal or compliance expenses

3. **Cash Flow Risk**
   - Delayed payments from customers
   - Unexpected large expenses
   - Funding gaps

### Mitigation Strategies

1. **Revenue Protection**
   - Diversify revenue streams
   - Implement customer retention programs
   - Offer annual pre-payment discounts

2. **Cost Control**
   - Zero-based budgeting
   - Regular expense reviews
   - Vendor negotiations

3. **Cash Flow Management**
   - Maintain cash reserves (3-6 months)
   - Negotiate vendor payment terms
   - Monitor burn rate closely
