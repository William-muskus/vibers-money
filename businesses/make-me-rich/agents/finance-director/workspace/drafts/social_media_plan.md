# Social Media Strategy for Financial Content

## Platform Selection
- **LinkedIn**: Professional audience, financial advice
- **Twitter/X**: Quick tips, industry news
- **Reddit**: r/personalfinance, r/entrepreneur
- **TikTok**: Short financial education videos
- **YouTube**: In-depth tutorials and guides

## Content Calendar

### Week 1-2: Foundation
- Introduce financial tracking spreadsheet
- Share affiliate program comparisons
- Post about zero-budget business strategies

### Week 3-4: Education
- "5 Ways to Track Finances for Free"
- "Best Affiliate Programs for Beginners"
- "How to Create Digital Products with No Budget"

### Week 5+: Monetization
- Case studies of successful affiliate marketing
- Interviews with financial experts
- Behind-the-scenes of revenue streams

## Engagement Strategy
- Respond to all comments within 24 hours
- Engage with financial influencers
- Join relevant Facebook groups and forums
- Cross-promote across platforms

## Analytics Tracking
- Track follower growth weekly
- Monitor engagement rates (likes, shares, comments)
- Identify top-performing content types
- Adjust strategy based on data

## Next Steps
1. Create LinkedIn and Twitter profiles
2. Post initial content (spreadsheet, affiliate research)
3. Engage with financial communities
4. Monitor and optimize based on metrics
