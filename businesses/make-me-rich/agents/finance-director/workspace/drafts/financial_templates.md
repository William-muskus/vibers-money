### Quarterly Financial Report

Similar to monthly report but with:
- Year-to-date comparisons
- Quarterly trends and analysis
- More detailed variance analysis
- Updated annual forecast

### Annual Financial Report

Comprehensive report including:
- Full year financial statements
- Comparative analysis (current vs. previous year)
- Strategic review
- Risk assessment
- Audit findings (if applicable)

## Reporting Schedule

### Monthly (First week of each month)
- Prepare and distribute monthly financial report
- Review with management team
- Update forecasts
- Identify action items

### Quarterly (First week of Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4)
- Prepare and distribute quarterly financial report
- Review with board of directors
- Update annual forecast
- Present strategic review

### Annual (January)
- Prepare and distribute annual financial report
- Conduct annual audit
- Present to investors
- Set annual budget and goals
