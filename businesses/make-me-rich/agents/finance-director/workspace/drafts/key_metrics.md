# Key Financial Metrics and KPIs

## Date: 2024-03-01

## Financial Metrics Framework

### Revenue Metrics

1. **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)**
   - **Definition**: Total predictable revenue from subscriptions per month
   - **Formula**: Sum of all active subscription plans
   - **Target**: $120,000 (Year 1)
   - **Monitoring**: Track daily/weekly to identify trends
   - **Importance**: Core metric for subscription businesses

2. **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)**
   - **Definition**: Annualized version of MRR
   - **Formula**: MRR × 12
   - **Target**: $1,440,000 (Year 1)
   - **Monitoring**: Quarterly review
   - **Importance**: Long-term revenue visibility

3. **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)**
   - **Definition**: Average revenue generated per customer
   - **Formula**: Total Revenue / Total Customers
   - **Target**: $120 (Year 1)
   - **Monitoring**: Monthly tracking by customer segment
   - **Importance**: Measures value per customer

4. **Revenue Growth Rate**
   - **Definition**: Percentage increase in revenue over time
   - **Formula**: (Current Period Revenue - Previous Period Revenue) / Previous Period Revenue × 100
   - **Target**: 20% month-over-month (Year 1)
   - **Monitoring**: Monthly comparison
   - **Importance**: Tracks business growth trajectory

### Customer Metrics

5. **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**
   - **Definition**: Cost to acquire a new customer
   - **Formula**: (Total Sales & Marketing Expenses) / (New Customers Acquired)
   - **Target**: $150 (Year 1)
   - **Monitoring**: Monthly tracking by channel
   - **Importance**: Measures marketing efficiency

6. **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)**
   - **Definition**: Average revenue from a customer over their lifetime
   - **Formula**: (ARPU × Average Customer Lifespan) / Churn Rate
   - **Target**: $1,500 (Year 1)
   - **Monitoring**: Quarterly review
   - **Importance**: Predicts long-term customer value

7. **LTV:CAC Ratio**
   - **Definition**: Ratio of lifetime value to acquisition cost
   - **Formula**: LTV / CAC
   - **Target**: 10:1 (Year 1)
   - **Monitoring**: Quarterly review
   - **Importance**: Indicates business sustainability

8. **Churn Rate**
   - **Definition**: Percentage of customers who cancel in a period
   - **Formula**: (Customers Lost / Total Customers at Start) × 100
   - **Target**: <5% monthly
   - **Monitoring**: Weekly tracking
   - **Importance**: Measures customer retention

9. **Customer Retention Rate**
   - **Definition**: Percentage of customers retained over time
   - **Formula**: (1 - Churn Rate) × 100
   - **Target**: >95% monthly
   - **Monitoring**: Monthly tracking
   - **Importance**: Complement to churn rate

10. **Net Revenue Retention**
    - **Definition**: Revenue retained from existing customers plus expansion revenue
    - **Formula**: (Revenue from Existing Customers + Expansion Revenue) / Revenue from Existing Customers
    - **Target**: >100% (indicates growth from existing customers)
    - **Monitoring**: Quarterly review
    - **Importance**: Measures upsell/cross-sell success

### Profitability Metrics

11. **Gross Margin**
    - **Definition**: Revenue minus cost of goods sold
    - **Formula**: (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue × 100
    - **Target**: 80% (Year 1)
    - **Monitoring**: Monthly tracking
    - **Importance**: Measures product profitability

12. **Operating Margin**
    - **Definition**: Operating income as percentage of revenue
    - **Formula**: (Operating Income / Revenue) × 100
    - **Target**: 20% (Year 3)
    - **Monitoring**: Quarterly review
    - **Importance**: Measures operational efficiency

13. **Net Margin**
    - **Definition**: Net income as percentage of revenue
    - **Formula**: (Net Income / Revenue) × 100
    - **Target**: 15-20% (Year 3)
    - **Monitoring**: Quarterly review
    - **Importance**: Overall profitability measure

14. **Burn Rate**
    - **Definition**: Rate at which cash is being spent
    - **Formula**: (Monthly Operating Expenses - Monthly Revenue)
    - **Target**: $40,000/month (Year 1)
    - **Monitoring**: Weekly tracking
    - **Importance**: Cash flow management

15. **Cash Runway**
    - **Definition**: Time until cash runs out at current burn rate
    - **Formula**: Cash Balance / Burn Rate
    - **Target**: 6-12 months
    - **Monitoring**: Weekly tracking
    - **Importance**: Funding and survival planning

### Efficiency Metrics

16. **Sales and Marketing Efficiency**
    - **Definition**: CAC relative to revenue generated
    - **Formula**: CAC / ARPU
    - **Target**: <12 months payback period
    - **Monitoring**: Monthly tracking
    - **Importance**: Measures marketing ROI

17. **R&D Efficiency**
    - **Definition**: R&D spend relative to revenue
    - **Formula**: R&D Expenses / Revenue
    - **Target**: 30-40% (Year 1)
    - **Monitoring**: Quarterly review
    - **Importance**: Innovation investment level

18. **G&A Efficiency**
    - **Definition**: G&A spend relative to revenue
    - **Formula**: G&A Expenses / Revenue
    - **Target**: 10-15% (Year 3)
    - **Monitoring**: Quarterly review
    - **Importance**: Operational efficiency

19. **Customer Support Efficiency**
    - **Definition**: Support cost relative to revenue
    - **Formula**: Support Expenses / Revenue
    - **Target**: 5-10% (Year 3)
    - **Monitoring**: Quarterly review
    - **Importance**: Customer service cost management

### Cash Flow Metrics

20. **Operating Cash Flow**
    - **Definition**: Cash generated from operations
    - **Formula**: Net Income + Depreciation + Amortization + Changes in Working Capital
    - **Target**: Positive by Year 2
    - **Monitoring**: Monthly tracking
    - **Importance**: Core cash generation measure

21. **Free Cash Flow**
    - **Definition**: Cash available after operating expenses and capital expenditures
    - **Formula**: Operating Cash Flow - Capital Expenditures
    - **Target**: Positive by Year 3
    - **Monitoring**: Monthly tracking
    - **Importance**: Cash available for growth and dividends

22. **Days Sales Outstanding (DSO)**
    - **Definition**: Average time to collect payments
    - **Formula**: (Accounts Receivable / Revenue) × Number of Days
    - **Target**: <30 days
    - **Monitoring**: Monthly tracking
    - **Importance**: Receivables management

23. **Days Payable Outstanding (DPO)**
    - **Definition**: Average time to pay suppliers
    - **Formula**: (Accounts Payable / COGS) × Number of Days
    - **Target**: 30-60 days
    - **Monitoring**: Monthly tracking
    - **Importance**: Working capital management

24. **Cash Conversion Cycle (CCC)**
    - **Definition**: Time from investment to cash collection
    - **Formula**: DSO + Inventory Days - DPO
    - **Target**: <30 days
    - **Monitoring**: Monthly tracking
    - **Importance**: Working capital efficiency

### Alerts and Thresholds

**Red Alerts (Immediate Action Required)**
- Churn Rate > 7%
- CAC > $200
- LTV:CAC < 5:1
- Gross Margin < 70%
- Cash Runway < 3 months
- Burn Rate > $50,000/month

**Yellow Alerts (Investigation Required)**
- Churn Rate > 5%
- CAC > $175
- LTV:CAC < 7:1
- Gross Margin < 75%
- Cash Runway < 6 months
- Burn Rate > $45,000/month

**Green Zone (Healthy)**
- Churn Rate < 5%
- CAC < $150
- LTV:CAC > 10:1
- Gross Margin > 80%
- Cash Runway > 6 months
- Burn Rate < $40,000/month
