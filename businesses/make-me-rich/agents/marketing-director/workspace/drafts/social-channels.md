1. **Facebook** - Community groups, broader reach

## Content Distribution Strategy

### LinkedIn
- **Posting Frequency**: 3-5 times per week
- **Content Types**:
  - Thought leadership articles
  - Industry insights
  - Company updates
  - Employee spotlights
  - Case studies
- **Best Times**: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM or 12-2 PM

### Twitter/X
- **Posting Frequency**: 3-5 times per day
- **Content Types**:
  - Quick tips and quotes
  - Industry news and commentary
  - Engagement questions
  - Polls and surveys
  - Retweets and replies
- **Best Times**: Weekdays, 9 AM-4 PM

### Instagram
- **Posting Frequency**: 3-5 times per week
- **Content Types**:
  - Carousel posts (tips, guides)
  - Infographics
  - Behind-the-scenes content
  - User-generated content
  - Stories (daily engagement)
- **Best Times**: Weekdays, 9 AM-12 PM or 5-7 PM

### YouTube
- **Posting Frequency**: 1-2 times per week
- **Content Types**:
  - Tutorials and how-to videos
  - Webinars and masterclasses
  - Customer success stories
  - Q&A sessions
  - Behind-the-scenes content
- **Best Times**: Weekdays, 2-4 PM

## Community Management Strategy

### Engagement Guidelines
- **Response Time**: 24 hours maximum for all platforms
- **Tone**: Friendly, professional, helpful
- **Engagement Rules**:
  - Like and respond to all comments
  - Answer questions thoroughly
  - Address negative feedback privately first
  - Encourage user-generated content
  - Tag relevant users and influencers

### Hashtag Strategy
- **Brand Hashtags**: #makeMeRich #WealthBuilding #TurnIdeasIntoIncome
- **Industry Hashtags**: #Entrepreneurship #SideHustle #FinancialFreedom #WealthMindset
- **Trending Hashtags**: Monitor and use relevant trending topics

### Growth Strategy
- **Follow/Unfollow**: Follow potential customers and influencers
- **Collaborations**: Partner with complementary brands
- **Influencer Marketing**: Identify and engage micro-influencers
- **Paid Promotion**: Boost high-performing organic content
- **Cross-Promotion**: Promote channels across platforms
