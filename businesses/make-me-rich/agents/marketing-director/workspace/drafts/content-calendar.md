# Content Calendar - make-me-rich

## Overview
30-day content calendar aligned with brand identity and launch objectives. Focus on building awareness, establishing credibility, and driving engagement.

## Content Themes
1. **Mindset & Motivation** - Overcoming psychological barriers to wealth
2. **Strategy & Planning** - Actionable wealth-building strategies
3. **Tools & Resources** - Introducing make-me-rich features
4. **Success Stories** - Social proof and inspiration
5. **Community Building** - Engaging and growing the audience

## Content Repurposing Strategy
- Blog posts → Social media excerpts → Email newsletters
- Videos → YouTube + LinkedIn + Twitter clips
- Infographics → Social media + Blog embeds
- Webinars → Recorded videos + Blog posts + Social clips
- Quizzes → Social media polls + Blog interactive elements

## Content Ownership
- **Marketing Director**: Strategy, creation, publishing
- **Content Creators**: Video production, graphic design
- **Community Manager**: Engagement, user-generated content
- **CEO**: Approvals, key messaging, vision alignment

## Performance Tracking
- **Weekly Reviews**: Every Friday at 3PM
- **KPI Dashboard**: Track engagement, reach, conversions
- **Adjustments**: Modify content based on performance data
- **Reporting**: Monthly report to CEO with insights and recommendations
